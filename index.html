Who We Are

The Church of Pentecost U.S.A., Inc. - James McKeown Worship Center in the Norcross District, is a place where we worship God in Spirit and Truth and live our lives according to the unadulterated Word of God. It is our earnest desire that you come to place your hope and trust in Jesus Christ as your Lord and personal Savior and also allow Him to lead you to grow daily in your relationship and walk with God.
